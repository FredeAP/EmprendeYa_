It is very easy to dream thanks to our imagination, but much more complex to turn it into something viable. How many people do you need, what amount of money should you invest or what needs would it cover?

Know the age, geographical location, interests and consumption habits of consumers who have companies in the same sector.

When you are clear that you want to undertake what you have to do is create a business plan in which an explanation of the product and service is contained.
